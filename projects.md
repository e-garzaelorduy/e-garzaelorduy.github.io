# Projects

## Capstone Project: Deep Learning Wrappers and Data Imputation Techniques
Under the guidance of Dr. Umberto Mignozzetti, my capstone project focuses on deep learning methodologies, including neural network structures, training processes, and practical applications. This exploration involves analyzing and refining various model architectures, gaining hands-on experience with neural network optimization and deployment in real-world scenarios.

As part of this project, I’m using Amelia II to perform multiple imputations on datasets with missing values. This approach addresses data gaps and enhances model accuracy, providing insights into effective handling of incomplete data in research contexts. Together, these components of the capstone deepen my technical expertise in both deep learning and advanced data imputation methods.

## Quick Decisions Replication Project
As part of my coursework, I’m replicating the findings from Quick Decisions by Critcher, Inbar, & Pizarro (2013). This project focuses on examining how moral judgments are influenced by decision speed, closely following the original study’s methodology to assess the robustness of its findings.
