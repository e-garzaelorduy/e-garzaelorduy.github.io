About Me

I’m Erika Garza-Elorduy, a master's student in Computational Social Science at UC San Diego with a background in Child Psychology. My work lives at the intersection of technology, education, and policy, where I use data-driven methods to better understand — and shape — how people and institutions interact with emerging technologies.

I’m especially passionate about AI ethics, digital literacy, and education reform. Whether I’m building tools, designing curriculum, or analyzing social systems, I’m driven by a commitment to responsible tech that centers agency, meaning, and transparency.

Beyond academics, I contribute to projects and organizations that align with my values, including ethical AI initiatives and youth-centered innovation networks. My goal is to make complex ideas approachable — and actionable — through thoughtful research and design.
